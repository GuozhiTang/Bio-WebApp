MEAN Stack App for Manufacturing

This is a MEAN Stack App(MongoDB/Mongoose, Express, Angular-CLI, NodeJS) including functionalities:

Login/Logout, Register, Dashboard, Transfer porcess, Grab and show specific components.

 

Instructions:

Register

Click the register button to fill all the information correctly, especially the email format. Then you have the access to login.

Login/Logout

After resgister successfully, go to login page to login. The dashboard of your account can been seen after that. Whenever you want to logout the account, just click the logout button.

Transfer

After login to your account, you can access the transfer process by clicking transfer button. You need to type in the barcode of the target plate first and then modify it. After that, type in the barcode of source plate with the volume you want to transfer. Finally, after modification, click submit to submit the whole transfer process.

Components

Show the exact data for each component at remote data server and can grab and show all the data to the local database. Show the search functionalities for each component. Meanwhile, "add" functionalities are settled but not allowed for users right now in order to protect the robot.
